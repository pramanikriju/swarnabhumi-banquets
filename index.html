Swarna Bhumi

A soiree of sorts with the fragrance laded air at the Swarna Bhumi Banquets

Swarna Bhumi- a space lavishly adorned with victorian chandeliers recapturing the nostalgic past, carpeted with rare intricate & illustrious Persian patterns & Italian marbles for a royal finish that exudes as sense of grandeur!


Swarna Bhumi is a space that serves to showcase, celebrate & bring to life moments close to the heart & soul.

Inaugurated in the year 2019, Swarna Bhumi is a repository of the finest things in life- a blend of rich, cultural heritage with modern state-of-the-art amenities, this landmark is a space for guests to enjoy the beautiful facets of life amidst the verdant ambience.
