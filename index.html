About Us

A soiree of sorts with the fragrance laded air at the Swarna Bhumi Banquets

Swarnabhumi- a space lavishly adorned with victorian chandeliers recapturing the nostalgic past, carpeted with rare intricate & illustrious Persian patterns & Italian marbles for a royal finish that exudes as sense of grandeur!

Swarnabhumi is a space that serves to showcase, celebrate & bring to life moments close to the heart & soul.

Inaugurated in the year 2019 by , Swarnabhumi is a repository of the finest things in life- a blend of rich, cultural heritage with modern state-of-the-art amenities, this landmark is a space for guests to enjoy the beautiful facets of life amidst the verdant ambience.

To define the future of hospitality by enriching the lives of our guests, clients and community by creating extraordinary experiences.
